Welcome to LiveHacking Awareness

Understanding how hackers exploit Wi-Fi vulnerabilities is the first step in securing your digital space. Explore our research, techniques, and security measures to stay protected.

Hackers can take advantage of various techniques like:

Wi-Fi Sniffing

Learn how attackers intercept data packets and how encryption prevents data theft.

Location Tracking

Understand how Wi-Fi signals can reveal your movement patterns inside your home.

Man-in-the-Middle Attacks

Discover how hackers intercept and alter network communication, and ways to mitigate risks.

Eavesdropping

Learn how Wi-Fi signals can be used to track the physical movement of devices within a house.

The Goal:

The goal of this concept is to raise awareness about the risks of insecure Wi-Fi networks and to emphasize the importance of securing your home Wi-Fi with strong passwords, encryption, and regular monitering to ensure that no one is intruding on your network!
