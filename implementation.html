Here are some implementation strategies to make your network more secure!

Best Ways to Secure:

Use Strong Encryption and Update Regularly

Secure your Wi-Fi with WPA3 encryption to ensure data remains unreadable to attackers. Keep your router's firmware up-to-date to patch known vulnerabilities automatically.

Segment and Limit Network Access:

Isolate IoT devices (like cameras and smart assistants) on a guest network to prevent potential breaches from affecting sensitive devices. Turn off unnecessary devices to reduce overall network traffic and visibility.

Add Physical and Digital Cover:

Emit cover signals: Some advanced routers can generate noise to confuse attackers trying to detect motion through Wi-Fi. Use Wi-Fi-blocking materials (like RF shielding paint or films) if signal leakage is a concern, though this may impact signal quality.

Monitor Your Network:

Regularly check for unauthorized devices connected to your Wi-Fi. Use network monitoring tools or firewalls to detect unusual activity and ensure your network stays secure.
